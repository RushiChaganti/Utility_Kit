Close & Forget Tab

Default shortcut: Alt + Shift + W

You can customize this in Firefox settings.

Change the shortcut in Firefox:

  1. Click the button above to open Add-ons Manager.
  2. In the left sidebar, click Extensions.
  3. Click the gear icon (⚙) → Manage Extension Shortcuts.
  4. Find Close & Forget Tab (Firefox) and set your keys.

What this does:

  • Closes the current tab
  • Deletes that URL from history
  • Removes it from Recently Closed when possible
