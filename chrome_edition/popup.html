Close & Forget Tab

Default Shortcut: Alt + Shift + W

To change the shortcut in Chrome/Edge:

  1. Click the button below.
  2. Scroll down to Close & Forget Tab.
  3. Click in the shortcut box and press your new keys.

To change the shortcut in Firefox:

  1. Click the button below.
  2. In the sidebar, select Extensions.
  3. Click the gear ⚙ menu → Manage Extension Shortcuts.
  4. Find Close & Forget Tab and set your keys.
